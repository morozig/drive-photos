Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Before Sign In with Google

While you are browsing our website without ever signing in with your Google account only most general information may be collected. GitHub as a hosting provider may log your requests fot html pages of this website. It can read and store your Ip address, your browser version, your operating system name

After Sign In with Google

If you decided to sign in with your Google account and use our service then some information will be saved in your browser:

  • Your account as a third party cookies. It is required for Google Drive Api
  • List of 10 last opened files in your browser memory to let you resume viewing

You may manually clear your browser memory set by this website at any time. Or if you simply sign out, this memory will be cleared as well

Also you may clear cookies set by Google, but it will sign you out on this device from all Google services

Accessing Your Google Drive Data

By using this website you can only read and not modify or delete any existing data in your Google Drive

This website lets you browse your photos saved in your Google Drive in a continuous manner. When you choose some image with Google Picker or open one of last files this file is downloaded and shown by your browser. Then information and thumbnails of all images in current directory are downloaded and displayed in sidebar. Then files nearby to current are also preloaded for faster transition. When you reach the end of current dirrectory website will start downloading images in next directory. All these images are kept only in temporary memory of webpage and cleared on reload or when you choose next image with Google Picker.
